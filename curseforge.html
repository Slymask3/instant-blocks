By Jason at Minecraft Universe (v1.4.7)

 

Features

  • Adds 17 new Instant Blocks to the game.
  • Adds 5 new Instant Wands to the game.
  • Instant Blocks could be obtained by crafting, or found in chests.
  • Each Instant Block generates a structure or performs a certian function upon right-clicking with Instant Wands.
  • Creates a configuration file at: ".minecraft\config\instantblocks.cfg".
  • If you think this is a bit overpowered, you could disable crafting the InstantBlocks in the config file, and instead have to look for them in dungeon chests, village chests, etc.
  • Adds an Instant Blocks command (/instantblocks or /ib).

 

Instant Blocks

  • Instant Wooden House Block:
    • Creates a wooden house.
  • Instant Mining Ladder Block:
    • Creates a ladder down to layer 12.
  • Instant Glass Dome Block:
    • Creates a glass dome.
    • Useful for underwater.
  • Instant Farm Block:
    • Creates a farm with regular seeds.
    • Has a 5% chance to create a farm with carrot seeds.
    • Has a 5% chance to create a farm with potato seeds.
  • Instant Rainbow Skydive Block:
    • Creates a structure from layer 1 to 256 out of coloured wool.
    • Teleports you to the top when created.
  • Instant Grinder Block:
    • Creates an xp grinder.
    • Only works with zombies and skeletons.
    • Place the block above a zombie/skeleton spawner.
  • Instant Pool Block:
    • Creates a pool with two diving boards.
  • Instant Escape Ladder:
    • Creates a ladder to the surface.
    • Can only be activated underground.
  • Instant Water Block:
    • Fills in all air blocks to the sides and bottom of it with water blocks.
    • Creating too many water blocks will cause the game to crash. In servers, only the client will crash.
  • Instant Lava Block:
    • Fills in all air blocks to the sides and bottom of it with lava blocks.
    • Creating too many lava blocks will cause the game to crash. In servers, only the client will crash.
  • Instant Suction Block:
    • Sucks in all water/lava blocks around it.
    • Creates an Instant Water/Lava Block where it used to be.
    • Sucking in too many water/lava blocks will cause the game to crash. In servers, only the client will crash.
  • Instant Rail Block:
    • Creates 37 rails forward.
  • Instant Statue Block:
    • Creates a statue of any Minecraft player.
    • Has options to generate or not generate certain body parts.
    • Uses exact RGB values, but has an option to use vanilla Minecraft wool colors.
  • Instant Harvester Block:
    • Harvests renewable resources around the player in a default radius of 50 blocks.
    • Has options to harvest certain resources, and to replant.
  • Instant Light Block:
    • Lights up dark areas around the player in a default radius of 50 blocks.
  • Instant Schematic Block:
    • Generates structures from .schematic files from /schematics/ folder.
  • Instant Huge Tree Block:
    • Generates any type of tree.

 

Feel free to use this mod in your modpack.
